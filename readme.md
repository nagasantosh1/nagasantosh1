# 💫 About Me:
I am an AI/ML Engineer focused on building real-world intelligent systems that solve complex operational problems at scale. My work centers around Agentic AI, multi-agent architectures, and retrieval-augmented systems, where I design solutions that go beyond prototypes and deliver measurable business impact.<br><br>At AIONOS (client: IndiGo), I developed an AI-powered invoice generation system that automates national and international billing workflows using multi-agent orchestration, RAG, and dynamic validation. I have also contributed to airline systems such as AI-driven web check-in and seat upgrade platforms, working on backend microservices, async APIs, and scalable architectures.<br><br>I enjoy combining LLMs, knowledge graphs, and distributed systems to build intelligent, production-ready applications. My goal is to create systems that are not only smart, but also reliable, efficient, and scalable in real-world environments.<br>
